Welcome to our art store

Turn your favourite photos to amazing handmade pastel portraits. To do this, just follow these 5 easy steps:

  • 1. Choose your reference photos
    The first thing you should do is to choose which photographs do you want our portrait artists to turn into an amazing pastel portrait.
    Into a single portrat we could combine elements from different photos. More information about photos and formats you will find here.
  • 2. Send us your photographs and instructions via email
    After you've chosen your pictures, send them to us via email. In your email, you should also write us detailed instructions about your portrait - if you have any preferences such as "I want this person to be on the left, and this one on the right", tell us. You can do that here:





    • Within a day or two, we will contact you and tell you if the pictures are good for a portrait.

  • 3. Payment
    After we have sent you an email, confirming that we are ready to start working on your personal masterpiece, you just have to pay. You can do this either by Paypal or debit/credit card (scroll down).
    We will get notified that you have paid and as soon as we do, we will begin working on your order.
  • 4. Review
    It usually takes us from 2 to 7 days to draw your portrait. However, it depends on a few things - such as will the portrait be in Black and White or in Colour, if it's a single or a family portrat, etc. Once the painting from your photo is ready, we will contact you to review it online. Most of our customers are happy with their first preview. If you feel that any modifications should be made, we receive your feedback, make the requested modifications and send it back to you for confirmation. Only then is the custom painting is sent to you.
  • 5. Recieve
    Your masterpiece will be packaged carefully and shipped to you via courier in a secure custom made box.

    • If you have any questions, have a look at our FAQ page or contact us.
