Once you've placed an order, we will work closely with you to gather reference photos via email. Please send us your photos and instructions to masterpiecedirect@gmail.com

Additionally, you can ask us what would be the best medium for the photos that you have provided. For example, some photos are best suited for black & white pastel while others work better with colour pastels.

We are professional portrait artists with extensive experience in the portrait art. All portraits are handcrafted to perfection using only the finest of pastels and special Canson paper.

Delivery

Typically it takes between 2 and 3 weeks from placing an order to receiving your portrait, depending on the capacity of our artists. We will try our best to keep this indicative timeframe.

Portrait Size

The size of the portraits may vary up to a maximum size of 50x65 cm (20x25 inches) depending on the reference photos. As a rule of thumb, our artists will draw paintings whereby the head/shoulders of the characters are real life sized.
