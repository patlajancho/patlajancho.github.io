Welcome to our art store

Turn your favourite photos to handmade pastel portraits. Our Master Artists specialise in portraits from photographs. A pastel portrait makes a unique handmade gift for your loved ones.

How to make an order?

Just follow these 5 easy steps:

  • 1. Choose your reference photos.
    The first thing you should do is to choose which photographs do you want our portrait artists to turn into an amazing pastel portrait. Don't forget that into a single portrat we could combine elements from different photos. What should your photos be like, what format should they be and the answers to many other questions you will find here. If you can't make the choice between a few different options, you could send us all of them and we will give you an advise which one is the best one to get turned into a pastel portrat.
  • 2. Send us your photographs and instructions via email.
    After you've chosen your pictures, send them to us via email. You can do that by clicking here:
    Click here to send an email
    or just send an email to masterpiecedirect@gmail.com. In your email, you should also write us detailed instructions about your portrait - if you have any preferences such as "I want this person to be on the left, and this one on the right", tell us now because chances are it will be way too late after we have already started working on your masterpiece.
    Within a day, we will contact you and tell you if the pictures are good for a portrait.
  • 3. Payment
    After we have sent you an email, confirming that we are ready to start working on your personal masterpiece, you just have to pay. You can do this either by Paypal or debit/credit card. Click here to view our price list.
    We will get notified that you have paid and as soon as we do, we will begin working on your order.
  • 4. Review
    It usually takes us from 2 to 7 days to draw your portrait. However, it depends on a few things - how busy we are at the moment of the order, will the portrait be in Black and White or in Colour, if it's a single or a family portrat, etc. Once the painting from your photo is ready, we will contact you to review it online. Most of our customers are happy with their first preview. If you feel that any modifications should be made, we receive your feedback, make the requested modifications and send it back to you for confirmation. Only then is the custom painting is sent to you.
  • 5. Recieve
    Your masterpiece will be packaged carefully and shipped to you via courier in a secure custom made box. We will be able to give you more details about delivering once you have placed your order.

    • If you have any questions, have a look at our FAQ page or contact us via email.

    Delivery

    Typically it takes between 2 and 3 weeks from placing an order to receiving your portrait, depending on the capacity of our artists. We will try our best to keep this indicative timeframe.

    Portrait Size

    The size of the portraits may vary up to a maximum size of 50x65 cm (20x25 inches) depending on the reference photos. As a rule of thumb, our artists will draw paintings whereby the head/shoulders of the characters are real life sized.
