How do I choose the perfect pictures for a portrait?

✓ The Do's
A head-and-shoulders picture, good lighting and good quality

✗ The Don'ts
Bad angle, contrejour or low resolution


What if my photos are not of the highest quality?

Just use our free Photo Consultation Service, located on our Contacts page. Send the photos before you commission a portrait and we will then give you advice and let you know if they're suitable.


What is the turnaround time?

Having the portrait finished
It will take us up to 10 days to complete your portrait. As soon as it's ready, we will send you a photo so you could review it online.

Having the portrait delivered
Delivery will take between 1 and 3 weeks depending on the shipping destination.

HERE'SWHAT'S NEXT

You have commissioned your portrait? Awesome! Here are the next steps...

✓ Fill in the form above

1. Receive confirmation email
Within 24 hours we will send you an email confirming we received all the needed information to begin working on your personal masterpiece.

2. Go to Checkout
Then you just have to come back to this page and proceed to our secure Checkout (located below) - as soon as we receive a payment notification, we will start working on your portrait. You can pay with either PayPal or credit/debit card.

3. That's where the magic happens
And your very own artwork is finished. Handcrafted to perfection. We will send you a few in-progress photos (just like those in the right) of your portrait before it gets done.

4. Review the portrait online
To make sure you get what you want, we will share your finished artwork online with you to review and approve.
Most of our customers are happy with their first preview. You feel like any modifications should be made? - It's alright! - we receive your feedback, make the requested modifications and send it back to you for confirmation. Only then is the customised painting is sent to you.

5. Choose a frame & Receive.
After you've previewed your portrait finished, you might want to Choose the frame that matches the artwork best. Then your masterpiece will be packaged carefully and shipped to you.
