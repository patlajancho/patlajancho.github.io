Where and how your personal information is collected

It is not essential that you tell us who you are or reveal any personal information about yourself as you browse this Website. When you do provide us with your personal information on our Website or via e-mail, we store this information on our secure systems. Personal information refers to any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to: a first and last name, home, billing or other physical address, an e-mail address and any other information associated with the individual. Apart from your contact information, we may collect information about your purchases, billing address, shipping address, phone number or other contact information. We do not store credit card details nor do we share customer details with any 3rd parties. We may also further combine this information with information that is publicly available.

All the information we collect via the Website or through correspondence with you is used to operate and improve the service we offer you and to personalise our service. We are committed to using your personal information only for:

Other uses of your information

IP Address. When you visit our Website, MasterpieceDirect collects details of your IP address. This is done to help diagnose problems with our servers, analyse traffic trends, gather demographic information and track the date and duration of each session within our Website. Together with your personal information, your IP address may also be used to prevent fraud or abuse, customise your shopping experience, improve our website's design and usability, improve our customer service and to understand your preferences and interests.

Data Collection Devices, such as Cookies and Tags: There are some instances where MasterpieceDirect may collect data through cookies, web logs, tags and other monitoring technologies to improve your browsing and shopping experience on our website. 'Cookies' are a piece of text stored on a user's computer through their web browser. It can be used for authentication, storing site preferences, shopping cart contents and the identifier for a server-based session. 'Tags' refers to code that is embedded in a web page or e-mail and is usually invisible to the user but allows checking that a user has viewed the page or e-mail.

We use these tools to improve and measure our Website's usability, track visits from our affiliates and partners, measure our Website's performance and to customise your shopping experience, among other things.

You have the option on most popular browsers to decline the use of cookies if you wish to do so, however this may make it harder or impossible to use certain parts or features of our website.

By using this website you consent to the use the use of cookies.

Children under 13: MasterpieceDirect does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Should we discover that we have unknowingly done so we will immediately delete that data collected from our systems. We suggest you visit the FTC's website (www.ftc.gov) for guidelines on how you can protect your children's privacy online.
