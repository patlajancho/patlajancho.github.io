Who we are

Hi there! We are Anny and Miro and we have been painting portraits professionally for over 25 years. We have an outdoor studio in Albena, Bulgaria, which we use during the summer months. During the remainder of the year, we create beautiful portraits for our online customers from throughout the world. We specialise in pastel portraits but also truly enjoy other techniques such as aquarelle.

We create beautiful art

We believe that few gifts are as unique and irreplaceable as original art. Our art is beautiful and affordable and makes a truly personal gift for the most special of occasions.

We create most of our work using the finest dry pastels and professional portrait paper. Over the years, we have perfected our technique and our art is of exceptional quality.

We have thousands of happy customers

We have been painting portraits professionally for over 25 years! During this time, we've had thousands of happy customers. We love making people happy with our art and hope to reach as many people as possible. Most of our work comes through referrals from previous customers, so please do spread the word about us if you are happy with your portrait.
